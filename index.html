Does the Type of Beer Hops Really Matter?

The short answer is yes!

Hops are a key component to a beer's bitterness and taste, so the type of hops can have a significant impact on the flavor of a beer. You can think of wine grapes and hops in similar ways. Just like a Pinot Noir and Merlot have different flavor profiles, two beers can have completely different tastes based on the hops used to brew them.
